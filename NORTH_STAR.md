# North Star

**Status:** Draft  
**Version:** 0.1.0 Foundation  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-09  

## North Star Statement

UVDL becomes a reusable engineering language that helps teams design better digital products faster, with fewer mistakes and decisions grounded in validated knowledge rather than intuition.

## Success Criteria

UVDL succeeds if it helps teams:

1. Ship digital products faster.
2. Reduce redesign and rework.
3. Improve conversion and task completion.
4. Improve accessibility and performance.
5. Reuse validated decisions across projects.
6. Explain why an interface is designed a certain way.

## Long-Term Direction

UVDL should evolve from documentation into a knowledge system, then into tooling.

Possible future layers:

- UVDL Core;
- UVDL Knowledge;
- UVDL CLI;
- UVDL Linter;
- UVDL Compiler;
- UVDL Lab.

## Constraint

Every release must provide practical value.

A release that is intellectually interesting but not useful in real projects is not considered successful.
