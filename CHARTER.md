# UVDL Charter

**Status:** Draft  
**Version:** 0.1.0 Foundation  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-09  

## Purpose

Ultima Vox Design Language exists to reduce bad design decisions in digital product design.

Its purpose is to transform design decisions from intuition into structured, reusable, and testable knowledge.

## Motto

> Decisions before Components.

## Scope

UVDL includes:

- design language;
- decision model;
- knowledge model;
- design principles;
- semantic terminology;
- design tokens;
- components;
- patterns;
- templates;
- reference implementations;
- validation processes;
- future tooling such as CLI, linter, and compiler.

UVDL does not include:

- client-specific brand themes;
- marketing copy for specific projects;
- CMS-specific business logic;
- unvalidated research concepts as Core standards.

## Values

1. Truth over opinion.
2. Validation over assumption.
3. Simplicity over complexity.
4. Consistency over novelty.
5. Humans first.
6. Accessibility by default.
7. Performance matters.
8. Reuse before reinvention.

## Evolution

Every UVDL rule can be changed if practical validation shows a better solution.

Nothing becomes Core without documentation, review, and validation.

## First Reference Implementation

`mechaniki.ru` is the first planned project where UVDL principles will be tested in production context.
